# Key Papers

## Core Tooth Regeneration

1. **Murashima-Suginami et al. (2021)**. Anti-USAG-1 therapy for tooth regeneration through enhanced BMP signaling. *Science Advances*, 7(7), eabf1798.
   - [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.abf1798) | [PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7880588/)
   - Key finding: Single injection of anti-USAG-1 antibody induces whole new teeth in mice. Blocking BMP interaction (not Wnt) is sufficient.

2. **Murashima-Suginami et al. (2007)**. Rudiment incisors survive and erupt as supernumerary teeth as a result of USAG-1 abrogation.
   - [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17555714/)
   - Original discovery: USAG-1 knockout mice develop extra teeth from rescued rudimentary tooth germs.

3. **Dental pulp stem cells for periodontitis (2025)**. Multicenter RCT in China, 132 patients.
   - [Nature Signal Transduction and Targeted Therapy](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41392-025-02320-w)

## Human Tooth Development Atlas

4. **Pagella et al. (2023)**. Single-cell census of human tooth development enables generation of human enamel. *Developmental Cell*.
   - [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.devcel.2023.07.013)
   - First generation of enamel proteins from iPSCs, guided by single-cell atlas.

## AI Drug Discovery (Analogous Successes)

5. **Absci (2025-2026)**. De novo antibody design with Origin-1 platform.
   - [bioRxiv preprint](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.01.14.699389v1.full)
   - Two AI-designed antibodies in human trials (ABS-101, ABS-201).

6. **Insilico Medicine (2024)**. Rentosertib (ISM001-055) Phase IIa results.
   - [Nature Medicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40461817/)
   - First generative AI drug in Phase II. Target-to-Phase1 in <30 months.
